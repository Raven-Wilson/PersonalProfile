About Me

I am currently studying Software Development at TUS. I fell in love with coding after partaking in a Women in STEM program by Technovation in secondary school, where we would be guided on how to make our own app. I've always loved art so finding a new interest where I could express my creativity while being able to make something practical immediately let me know this was the career path for me.

My goal is to be a full stack developer when I'm finished college. I love the design elements of UX and the idea of being able to create an intricate backend along side it. I also want to be full stack because I like the idea of being able transition and work with different teams, whether they work with front or backend, within the company I work for in the future.
