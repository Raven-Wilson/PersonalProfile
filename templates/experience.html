Technovation Women in STEM

During secondary school, I participated in a technology entrepreneurship program that focused on introducing girls to the field of tech. We were put into teams of 4 with other girls in our year, where we would meet weekly to create an app prototype and business plan based on real-world issues. Our app idea was a platform where women could rate accomadation experience based on how safe they felt there. We had to design our app both on paper and using canva, where we then would be given advice on how to structure it in a user friendly way that wasn't visually overwhelming. We used MIT app inventor to create our app, so we weren't coding in the traditional sense but it did provide us a lot of knowledge on the concepts involved with building an app. I worked more on the front-end app set-up and some of the back-end drag and drop elements. We got to demonstrate it and pitch the idea in the Microsoft office in Dublin.
